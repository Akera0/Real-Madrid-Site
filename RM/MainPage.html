Biography

Founded on 6 March 1902 as Madrid Football Club, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception. The word Real is Spanish for Royal and was bestowed to the club by King Alfonso XIII in 1920 together with the royal crown in the emblem.

Trophies

Real Madrid's trophy cabinet gleams with approximately 35 La Liga titles, around 14 UEFA Champions League triumphs, and approximately 19 Copa del Rey victories. Globally, they've secured the FIFA Club World Cup about 4 times, complemented by around 4 UEFA Super Cup victories. These numbers succinctly capture the enduring legacy of Real Madrid's footballing dominance..
